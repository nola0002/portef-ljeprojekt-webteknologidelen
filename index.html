Book your car.

Reserve the nearest “Green”.

Reserve a car easily

You can find, reserve and unlock the car with the app. The app is the key to a thousand of electrical cars, and always shows you the nearest car, and its battery level. When you’ve decided on a car you can reserve it in the app. The first 20 min. of reservation are free per trip, per day. Thereafter, reservations are DKK 1/min.

Get going in your “Green”.

Unlock the car in the app.

It’s easy getting around in a car

Unlock your reserved car with a single swipe. The car has automatic gear and you start it by stepping on the brake and pressing the “start” button – on the right side of the steering wheel. Place the shift in “D” and you can drive. You can take the car out of the zone, as long as you make sure to bring it back to a valid parking spot.

Ending your trip.

Park easily and end your trip.

Free parking is always nearby

You can park at public parking spaces within our zone. If there is a time limit, there must be at least 2 hours parking limit 24/7 for you to park there. Always end your trip inside the zone. If you start the trip in Copenhagen, the trip must end within the zone of Copenhagen again. The same applies to Aarhus. Inside the zone, we have reserved a lot of VIP-parking spaces around. We call these Hotspots. You can find all the Hotspots in the app. Here you’ll always find easy and free parking.
